About Me

1 in 25

Native American women in the world independently building across both enterprise low-code platforms and full-stack API environments.

I started coding as a kid by breaking and fixing HTML and CSS pages, teaching myself through trial and error. Along the way, I learned web internals hands-on — exploring directory structures and image endpoints — which sparked a lifelong interest in secure, resilient systems. That curiosity grew into the technical foundation I carried into my professional life.

For over a decade, I worked in high-stakes operations — managing compliance, risk, and performance as an member of the operations management team. My role required keeping critical systems running without disruption, meeting local, state, and federal standards, and mentoring staff under servant leadership principles.

Now, I’ve returned to my first passion: building. I run my own business as a full-time developer and founder, blending enterprise IT experience with modern web technologies. My current focus spans web applications, SharePoint solutions, and high-performance computing (CUDA, parallel programming, and GPU acceleration). I bring a perspective that’s rare: grounded in sovereignty, driven by discipline, and future-focused.
